Taylor Machen

My name is Taylor Machen and I'm a Digital Innovations Student at Arkansas State University. I look at art as anything that is created with thought and emotion. This allows for many different things to be classified as art that might not normally be classified as it. I try my best to convey how a piece makes me feel or how I can posibly convey an emotion to a viewer of my art. Under this you will see some of my works that I've created over my time in the DI program at Arkansas State and a lot of the hard work and emotion that has gone into each piece. I hope you enjoy your look at what I've created and enjoy getting to possibly learn a bit about me!
